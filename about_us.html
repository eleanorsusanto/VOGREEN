ABOUT US

VORGREENS started as a plant-based catering in December 2017. Its purpose is to serve plant-based meals to people with specific dietary. The business itself grew larger and opened its first restaurant in June 2019. Currently, VORGREENS has more than 4 restaurant in different cities across Indonesia and hoping to grow more.

Our purpose is to create different variaties of plant-based meals for people to enjoy in ocassions. We also provided pleasure for meat eaters so that they could enjoy plant-based meals while giving a taste that is exactly the same as meat, yet healthy and more sustainable

OUR VISION

VORGREEENS supports a sustainable environment. Research has shown that consuming meat and animal products produces the highest carbon footprint that ruins the environment. Livestock farming produces from 20-50% of all man-made greenhouse gas emissions. By cutting out our animal products from our diet, it helps our world to be a better place and more sustainable.

We vision VORGREENS as a plant-based eatery that serves healthy and delicious meals. Research shows that a poor diet can cause serious health issues, such as diabetes, cancer, heart disease, and many more. We ensure you that our dishes are served with a lot of healthy benefits that prevents health issues.

OUR MISSION

Our mision is to make the world a more sustainable place. We make the best dishes that not only to save the environment, but also healthy yet delicious. With serving plant-based dishes, we inspire many people to also accomplish our mission.
